Gluten Free Peach Cobbler

  • Servings: 12
  • Prep Time: 1 hour
  • Cook Time: 30 min.
  • Total Time: 1 hour 30 min.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups canned, sliced peaches in fruit juice
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 8 Tbsps. butter
  • 1 1/2 cups GF self-rising flour
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • Sprinkle of ground cinnamon

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Combine peaches, brown sugar, and water in a medium saucepan and mix well. Bring to a boil and then simmer for ten minutes.
  3. Remove filling from heat.
  4. Put butter in a 3 quart baking dish and place in the preheated oven to melt.
  5. Mix sugar, *flour and milk slowly together to prevent clumping. Pour mixture over melted butter but DO NOT stir.
  6. Pour fruit on top of batter, gently pouring in syrup.
  7. Sprinkle top with ground cinnamon to taste. Batter will rise to the top while baking. Bake 30 to 45 minutes.
  8. To serve, spoon onto a plate with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.

Note: *If you don't have access to self-rising GF flour, you can make your own by adding 1tsp. baking powder and 1/4 tsp. salt for every cup of all purpose flour. For my flour blend, I generally use 1 cup Krsuteaz GF flour and 1/2 cup Bob's Red Mill Baking flour for the best crust consistency.

Gluten Free Pumpkin Bread

  • Servings: 2 1/2 loafs
  • Prep Time: 15 min
  • Cook Time: 1 hr
  • Total Time: 1 hr 15 min

Ingredients

  • 3 1/2 cups *GF flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 2 Teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 3 cups sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon allspice
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 can pumpkin

Directions

  1. Mix together oil and canned pumpkin in a large bowl.
  2. Add sugar and then eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition.
  3. Remove filling from heat.
  4. Add flour and remaining ingredients together in a separate bowl, stirring well before adding to the wet ingredients 1/2 cup at a time until well combined.
  5. Butter and flour loaf pans. Fill about half way with batter.
  6. Bake about 1 hour at 350 F.
  7. Recipe makes two regular sized loafs and one mini.

Note: *GF Flour is 2 1/2 cups Krusteaz GF Flour and 1 cup of Bob's Red Mill GF Baking Flour.
