BBQ Green Beans

  • Servings: 8-12
  • Prep Time: 15 min.
  • Cook Time: 30 min.
  • Total Time: 45 min.

Ingredients

  • 4 (14 oz.) cans of green beans
  • 1 medium sweet onion, dices
  • 5 strips of bacon
  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 1 cup brown sugar

Directions

  1. Mix ketchup, diced onion, and brown sugar together for the topping.
  2. Cook bacon and chop or crumble when done. (save the bacon grease)
  3. Pour the bacon grease into the casserole dish.
  4. Dump the green beans into the dish, pour on the topping, then sprinkle with crumbled bacon.
  5. Bake at 350 F for 30 minutes.
