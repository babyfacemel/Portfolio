Bottom

The Pleco fish is super popular due to it's scavenging hunger. It does great in fish aquariums! Mainly an algae eater but the Pleco will also feed on meaty foods. They are sometimes called the 'garbage disposal' due to the wide variety foods and clean up process they due on the bottom of fish aquariums.

Albino Pleco

Albino Bristlenose Pleco is a mostly brown fish marked with light spots. They can blend in the shore and rock bottom thanks to their light-yellow or white appearance. Thanks to their whiskers on their mouth/nose it is a lot easier for them to find food. This species grows with a maximum size of 5 inches.

Chocolate Pleco

Our small Chocolate Albino Pleco algae eating fish grow to 1.5 inches in size. This fish has a typical lifespan of 10-15 years and thrive best in water temperatures ranging from 73-82 degrees. The Chocolate Albino Pleco tends to stick to the bottom or sides of the tank.

Zebra Pleco

Zebra pleco. The zebra pleco (Hypancistrus zebra) is a species of catfish endemic to Brazil where it occurs in the Big Bend area of the Xingu River, a tributary of the Amazon River, and was first described in the early 1990s. It gets its name from its black and white stripes, resembling the colouration of a zebra.
